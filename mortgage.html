Calculator Purpose Description
Monthly Mortgage Payment A calculator that will tell you how much you will need to pay per month based on your mortgage.
15 vs 30 Year Mortgage Comp Creates a comparable between a 15 year and 30 mortgage showing the amount that you will need to pay monthly, and the amount that you will need to pay in interest over the course of the mortgage.
Fixed Rate vs ARM Mortgage Comp Creates a comparable between a fixed rate mortgage and an adjustable-rate mortgage (ARM).
Re-Finance Comp Compares an old mortgage with the Re-Financed mortgage numbers. Compares the payment due per month, and shows various information about the new re-financed mortgage including how much you will be saving or losing due to the new mortgage.
